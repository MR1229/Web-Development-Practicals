About DYPCET

D.Y. Patil College of Engineering & Technology in Kasaba Bawada, Kolhapur operates as a self-financing autonomous institution. Founded in 1984, the college has over four decades of excellence in technical education. The institution holds Autonomous status by UGC and Shivaji University, AICTE approval, Government recognition, and NAAC 'A' Grade accreditation.
