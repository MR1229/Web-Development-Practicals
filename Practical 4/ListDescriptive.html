Top Engineering Colleges (NIRF Ranking)

IIT Madras
Indian Institute of Technology Madras was established in 1959 with technical collaboration from Germany. It is located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. IIT Madras has consistently ranked 1st in the NIRF Engineering Rankings in recent years. The institute is known for high-quality research, innovation, and strong industry connections. It also has a good global presence and is ranked among the top engineering institutes in the world.
IIT Delhi
Indian Institute of Technology Delhi was established in 1961 and is located in New Delhi. It is one of the leading engineering institutes in India and frequently ranks within the top 3 in NIRF rankings. IIT Delhi is known for its research output, entrepreneurship culture, and strong academic programs. It also appears in global university rankings and has collaborations with many international institutions.
IIT Bombay
Indian Institute of Technology Bombay was established in 1958 with assistance from UNESCO and the Soviet Union. Located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, IIT Bombay is known for excellence in engineering, technology, and innovation. It regularly features in top positions in NIRF rankings and has a strong presence in global engineering rankings. The institute is also well known for startups and research.
IIT Kanpur
Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur was established in 1959 with support from a consortium of American universities. It is located in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. IIT Kanpur is known for its rigorous academic structure, scientific research, and technical education. It ranks among the top engineering colleges in India according to NIRF and also holds recognition in international rankings.
IIT Kharagpur
Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur was established in 1951 and is the first IIT in India. Located in West Bengal, it laid the foundation for technical education in the country. IIT Kharagpur consistently ranks among the top engineering institutions in India under NIRF rankings. It is known for its large campus, diverse academic programs, and contribution to engineering education.
